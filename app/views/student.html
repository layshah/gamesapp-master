Your Journey



BEGINS STEP 1

STEP 1: Start your Journey

You want to pursue or explore your interest
Pursue your interest through interest based internship. We believe that people are more successful and happy when they pursue their interest. We have designed a 3A's process which will help you to discover your interest

Example: If you are interested in designing then we will help you to get an internship in the design domain

APTITUDE Step 2

STEP 2: Aptitude

Your Natural Ability to do something
To help you figure out your strengths
Online Test to test your basic skills and gauge your passion

We do not conduct like other aptitude tests like maths english etc. We have a cool passion predictor test which will predict your passion in few minutes and then we have a basic domain knowledge test.

ALTITUDE Step 3

STEP 3: Altitude

An Transparent offline process to help you to do things related to your interest and have Fun
1. Be a part of interestship event
2. Connect with people of similar interest
3. Form a team
4. Build things related to your interest
5. Showcase your work

Example: You will come work on 2HR short projects in teams in the offline event

ATTITUDE Step 4

STEP 4: Attitude

Provide you with better chance of conversion so that company can see your skills and work as opposed to just a CV/Resume

Example: You will go for an interview with aptitude score and project done in altitude round
